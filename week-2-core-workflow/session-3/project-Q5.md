#Three Sentences Explaining HEAD and Detached HEAD
* HEAD is git's internal pointer and it points to your exact current location and active snapshot within your repository's historical timeline. 

* It points to the tip of a branch and it moves forward accordingly as you keep saving commits. 

* A detached HEAD means that you have used the command git checkout <hash> which moves you away from the HEAD and let's you explore ina read-only mode.
