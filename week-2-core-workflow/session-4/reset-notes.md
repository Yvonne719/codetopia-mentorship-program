#ANSWERS TO QUESTIONS FOR reset notes.md
 **A soft reset** is a command that takes your repoaitory back to a previous commit, but leaves
all your modified code completely untouched.

#DIFFERENCES BETWEEN soft, mixed and hard resets
|git command |Difference |
| : -----| :---------- |
|**soft reset** | It takes you back to a previous commit, but leaves all your modified code|
|**mixed reset** | Takes you to a previous commit, completely erases the recent records from your timeline|
|**hard reset** | Forcefully rewinds your repository's timeline to a past commit and erases everything since then|
